Where Does the Name "Moz" Come From?

Moz, formerly SEOmoz, has a name that was inspired by
organizations like DMOZ, Mozilla, and Chefmoz — all companies
that strove for engaging their communities with openness and
integrity. From the very beginning, we felt passionately about
bringing that same integrity to the frustratingly opaque SEO
industry.

Rand Fishkin

Founder at Moz
